The 23 Best Tunic Tops For Leggings

Tunics and leggings: Arguably the greatest combination since peanut butter and jelly. While any tunic (or top) can be worn with leggings, there is one key feature that makes some better than others, especially if you're headed somewhere a bit more formal like the office. For those more dressed-up environments, the best tunic tops for leggings will hit below the waist. These tops also come in all different sorts of styles and shapes.

How To Style Tunic Tops

Ahead, you'll find 23 stylish tunics that are perfect for pairing with leggings, but most of them will look great with jeans and denim cutoffs, too; some can even be worn as beach cover-ups or dresses. (Some styling tips: Try pairing the former with a sun hat and sandals, or dress up the latter with statement earrings and heels.) The beauty of the general tunic-and-legging combo is that it works with just about any type of footwear: chunky sandals in the summer, sneakers for more casual occasions, and boots in the fall. And for those tunics that are extra-long and billowy, you can give them some shape by cinching them at the waist with a belt.

All of the best tunic tops featured ahead are available on Amazon Fashion, and most of them cost less than $30 (though you will find some luxury picks from brands like Free People and Vince for equal measure). Scroll on to see them all!
