Mikey Cobban is a British singer known for being one of the members of the 5-member boyband RoadTrip. He is the 3rd youngest member of the band and has performed many covers of the popular singers like Ed Sheeran, Justin Bieber and more. Mikey has also been a part of band’s original tracks like I Wasn’t Worried, After The Show, and No No No.
