What was he known for?

Engmark’s notable voice roles included Mike Wazowski in Monsters University (2013), Knerten in Twigson in Trouble (2011) and Twigson Ties the Knot (2010), and he also participated in and won Skal vi danse in 2010.

When did Åsleik Engmark pass away?

He died on February 12, 2017, at the age of 51.

Where did he die?

Engmark passed away in Brussels, Belgium.

What was the cause of his death?

Was Åsleik Engmark married?
