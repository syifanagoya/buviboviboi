Lenny Kravitz Shares Affectionate Picture With Zoe, Ex-Wife Lisa Bonet

Blood pic.twitter.com/x08BdmM4Xi

— Lenny Kravitz (@LennyKravitz) May 6, 2015

Could this family be any more beautiful? Days after they turned heads at the 2015 Met Gala in New York City on May 4, Lenny Kravitz shared a photo of himself with daughter Zoe Kravitz and ex-wife Lisa Bonet via Twitter and Instagram.

The stunning black-and-white shot — taken by Mathieu Bitton — shows the “American Woman” singer, 50, and the Divergent actress, 26, flanking the Cosby Show alum, 47, and kissing her on the cheek. Lenny has his arm around Bonet’s shoulder, as she affectionately cups his chin with one hand. 

The couple, who wed in November 1987 and welcomed daughter Zoe in December 1988, have been divorced since 1993 but remain on good terms. (Clearly.) They even walked the Met Gala red carpet arm-in-arm on Monday, both clad in Valentino.

Bonet — who married Game of Thrones hunk Jason Momoa in 2007, and has two kids with him — made a statement in a long-sleeved floral gown from the designer’s Spring 2015 Haute Couture collection, while Lenny sported a dark pinstripe suit and sunglasses. Zoe, meanwhile, donned a custom Alexander Wang gown with Swarovski crystal embellishments.

Speaking about her family with Ocean Drive earlier this year, the Hollywood scion praised the dynamic between her divorced parents, saying they’re still “very close” even after two decades apart. “They have so much love for each other,” she shared. “My dad and my stepdad get along really well.” 

Of her half-siblings, Nakoa-Wolf, 6, and Lola, 7, she added, “My dad loves and is close with my brother and sister. It’s really beautiful. As long as everyone can be in the same room together, and have love for each other, it’s totally healthy.”
