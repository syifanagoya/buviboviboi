Josh Altmans Wife Heather Bilyeu Is Pregnant

First comes marriage, then comes baby! Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles stars Josh Altman and his wife, Heather Bilyeu, are expecting their first child together, Altman’s rep confirms to Us Weekly.

The Bravo personalities first announced they are going to be parents during an interview with Extra on Monday, September 12. 

“We’re expecting our first child! I just hit the second trimester,” Heather, clad in a form-fitting white dress, announced during the on-camera interview.

The luxury real estate agent also revealed the sex of their baby-to-be.

“[We are] having a little angel baby girl and I am going to spoil the hell out of her,” Altman, 37, told Extra’s Charissa Thompson.

“I’m out the door now, I don’t even matter,” Bilyeu joked.
