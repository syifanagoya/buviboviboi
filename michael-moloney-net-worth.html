What is Michael Moloney's Net Worth?

Michael Moloney is an American decorator and television personality who has a net worth of $3 million. Michael Moloney was born in Palos Verdes, California in December 1963. Moloney is best known for being a designer and starring on the reality television series Extreme Makeover: Home Edition from 2003 to 2012.

He has appeared on the TV series Dancing with the Stars, Who Wants to Be a Millionaire, and The Great Christmas Light Fight. He has also regularly appeared on the TV series Clean House on the Style Network and has also appeared on the View, EXTRA!, Entertainment Tonight, Designer's Challenge, Less than Perfect, Access Hollywood, and Fashion Wrap. Moloney worked in the fashion industry for 10 years before working as an interior designer.
