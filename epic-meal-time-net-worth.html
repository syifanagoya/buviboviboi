What is Epic Meal Time's Net Worth?

Epic Meal Time is a Canadian cooking show and web video series that has a net worth of $8 million dollars. Epic Meal Time is probably most famous for operating a very popular Youtube channel where they feature videos of the team creating extravagant meals. The show is both comedy and how-to program and features Harley Morenstein, Alex Perrault, Tyler Lemco, David Heuff, Josh Elkin, and Ameer Atari. The group of friends cooks very high calorie foods, with such ingredients as bacon and Jack Daniel's. The group launched their show after they posted a video of Morenstein eating a six-patty, 18-bacon-strip cheeseburger at Wendy's, that subsequently accrued tens of thousands of views.

Modeled after the stunt show, "Jackass", the series shows the friends eating fatty, high calorie meals that they design themselves, usually combining multiple fast food items and alcohol. As of this writing, their youtube channel has nearly 7 million subscribers and more than 1 billion video views. In addition to splitting revenue with ads that are shown on their videos, Epic Meal Time has a popular line of T-Shirts, a video game, a cookbook and an iPhone App. Their success has allowed both Morenstein, a former teacher, and Toth, a former graphic designer, to quit their jobs to focus full time on their budding media empire.
