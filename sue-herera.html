CNBC reporter Sue Herera shares three Children with Husband Daniel Herrera

Sue was born as Susan McMahon on 15th November 1957 in Spokane, Washington, United States. Moreover, she holds American citizenship and hails from a white ethnicity/race. Apart from this, she is a married woman.
