Who Is Kurt Russell? Is He Married To Goldie Hawn? Net Worth, &amp; Career

Kurt Russell is an American actor who has starred in several Disney movies such as Dennis the Menace (1962), The Dick Powell Theatre (1962), Sam Benedict (1963), The Eleventh Hour (1963), and so on. He achieved 'Star on the Walk of Fame' on 4 May 2017, at 6201 Hollywood Blvd, and in 2020, he became the hall of 'Great Western Performers.'

Some of his notable works include Once Upon a Time...in Hollywood (2019), Crypto (2019), and The Christmas Chronicles (2018).

Let's find more about Kurt Russell.

Is Kurt Russell Married To Goldie Hawn?

Kurt Russell is currently in a relationship with Goldie Hawn since 1983. Even though the couple is in a long-term relationship, they aren't married.

In the 2007 interview with Woman'sDay, Kurt's girlfriend Goldie explained why they aren't married. The American actress explained:

"We have done just perfectly without marrying. I already feel devoted, and isn't that what marriage is supposed to do? So as long as my emotional state is in a state of devotion, honesty, caring, and loving, then we're fine. We have raised our children brilliantly; they are beautiful people. We did a great job there and we didn't have to get married to do that. I like waking up every day and seeing that he is there and knowing that I have a choice. There is really no reason to marry."

Likewise, Kurt echoed the same sentiment in his 2017 interview with Extra:

"Look, I’ve said this for 34 years, I don’t think Goldie and I, in real terms, are any different than any other couple; we go through all the same things. But the important thing of who the two people are in a relationship is what you have to deal with. There’s going to be ups and downs and sideways and everything else. I just think after 34 years, you’re going to have experienced every emotion that you can together."

Talking about their romantic encounter, Russell and Hawn first met in the Disney movie The One and Only, Genuine, Original Family Band in 1966. They were spotted together on the filming location of the movie Swift Swing in 1983. Russell took Hawn to The Playboy Club, where he says they talked for hours, and then they two decided to continue the fun at a house Hawn had recently purchased to renovate.

Previously Kurt Russell was married to Season Hubley in 1978 and got a divorce in 1983. The ex-pair welcomed a son named Boston Russell on 16 February 1980.

Before dating Russell, Hawn already had two children, Kate Hudson, born in April 1979, and Oliver Hudson, born in September 1976, from her second ex-husband, Bill Hudson. They were married from 1976 to 1982. She was previously married to Gus Trikonis from 1969 to 1976. In July 1986, Russell and Hawn welcomed their first son named Wyatt Russell from their relationship.

Kurt Russell Net Worth And Career

Kurt Russell has a net worth of $100 million throughout his career. Kurt raises his stardom from the child actor to the A-list actor.

He starred in different TV series and movies such as Escape from L.A. (1996), Big Trouble in Little China (1986), Now You See Him, Now You Don't (1972),The Man from U.N.C.L.E.(1964), Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017), The Fate of the Furious (2017), Deepwater Horizon (2016), Furious 7 (2015), Poseidon (2006), Sky High (2005), Interstate 60: Episodes of the Road (2002), and more.

Kurt Russell has won several awards. He won the Academy of Science Fiction, Fantasy & Horror Films, the USA for the life career Award in 2003. Kurt has also won Blockbuster Entertainment Awards for the Blockbuster Entertainment Award as a Favorite Actor-Aventure/Drama category in the movie Executive Decision(1996) in 1997. 

Apart from that, Kurt won Fangoria Chainsaw Awards for the Best Actor in the movie Bone Tomahawk (2015), Fangoria Horror Hall of Fame in 2016. He was the winner of the Hollywood Film Awards in the Ensemble of the Year in the movie The Hateful Eight (2015).

Who Is Kurt Russell?

Kurt Russell was born to parents Bing Russell and Louise Russell on 17 March 1971. He hails from Thousand Oaks, California. Russell's father, Bing Russell, is an actor.

In 1969, Kurt graduate from Thousand Oaks High School. He was a baseball player all through his high school and college days. In early 1970, he played for the California Angels minor league, the Bend Rainbows, Walla Walla Islanders, Portland Mavericks, and El Paso Sun Kings. Kurt suffered shoulder injuries after a baseball match, following which he retired from baseball.

49-years-old Kurt Russell has a height of 5 feet 9.5 inches or 1.759 m.
