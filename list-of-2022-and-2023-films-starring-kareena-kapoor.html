Early life and Background

Bebo Kapoor is the younger daughter of actors Randhir Kapoor and Babita, and she was born on September 21, 1980 in Bombay (which is now known as Mumbai). Her older sister Karisma is also an actress. She is the maternal granddaughter of actor Hari Shivdasani, the paternal granddaughter of actor Raj Kapoor, and the great-granddaughter of director Prithviraj Kapoor. Raj Kapoor is her maternal grandfather, while Prithviraj Kapoor is her paternal grandfather. Her uncle, the actor Rishi Kapoor, has the same profession as her cousin, the actor Ranbir Kapoor, who is also an actor. According to Kapoor, her mother read the book Anna Karenina while she was pregnant with her, and that’s where the name “Kareena” came from. On her father’s side, she is of Punjabi and Hindu descent, while on her mother’s side, she is of Sindhi and Hindu lineage, along with some British ancestry.

Kapoor, who once described herself as a mischievous and spoiled child, became interested in acting after watching movies beginning at an early age. In particular, the performances of actresses Nargis and Meena Kumari served as a source of motivation for her. Despite the fact that she came from a film family, her father was against the idea of women working in the film industry. He considered that doing so was incompatible with the traditional roles of caregiving and responsibility that fall to women in the home. This resulted in a disagreement between her parents, and it wasn’t until October 2007 that they were able to patch things up and live together again. She was brought up by her mother, who held a number of jobs in order to provide for her kids up until the time that Karisma made her debut as an actress in 1991.
