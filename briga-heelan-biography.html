Who is Briga Heelan?

Briga Heelan is an American actress and comedian who is famous for her work on the sitcoms Ground Floor’, ‘Cougar Town’, ‘Undateable’, ‘Love’ and ‘Great News’.

Briga Heelan: Age, Parents, Siblings, Family

She was born on April 2, 1987, in Andover, Massachusetts, USA. She is currently 35 years old.

Her father’s name is Kevin Heelan and her mother’s name is Kimball Heelan. Her father is a  playwright, dramatist, and faculty member at Philips Andover Academy since 1983 and her mother is an actress and Lowell Public Schools, teacher.

Her parents met for the first time when Jevin was getting his Master’s degree at Smith College.

Briga holds American citizenship and her ethnicity is English, Irish, German, French.

Briga Heelan: Education, School/College University

She joined Walnut Hill School for the Arts. She studied at the Cincinnati Conservatory of Music for a year. She transferred to the University of Southern California.

Briga Heelan: Professional Life and Career

She initially planned to attend the musical theatre and she met her co-star, Skylar Astin, at a musical theatre convention.

She became recognized after her TV role on ‘Coughar Town’. She played the recurring character on ‘Undateable’ and appears in three episodes plus the pilot.

She managed her time to shoot both Bill Lawrence-helmed ‘Ground Floor’ and ‘Undateable’ simultaneously. Her performance on ‘Ground Floor’ was reviewed positively by the TV critic Alan Sepinwall and by The Los Angeles Times.

Later, she played as Katherine/Kiki in the episode ‘Guess Who’s Coming to Decorate’ in ‘Truth Be Told’ in 2015, Connie Cavanaugh in the episode ‘That Text Was Not Meant for Josh’ in ‘Crazy Ex-Girlfriend’ in 2016, played as Heidi in 6 episodes of ‘Love’ from 2016 to 2017, And portrayed the lead role of Katie Wendelson in ‘Great News’ from 2017 to 2018.
